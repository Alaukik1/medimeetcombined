Frequently Asked Questions

How does MediMeet virtual consultation work?

MediMeet connects you with qualified healthcare professionals through secure video consultations. Simply book an appointment, join the virtual meeting at the scheduled time, and receive medical advice, prescriptions, and follow-up care as needed.

Is my medical information secure on MediMeet?

Absolutely. MediMeet is fully HIPAA-compliant and uses end-to-end encryption for all consultations. Your medical records and personal information are stored securely and are only accessible to authorized healthcare providers involved in your care.

Can doctors prescribe medication through MediMeet?

Yes, doctors on MediMeet can prescribe medications when medically appropriate. Prescriptions are sent electronically to your preferred pharmacy for pickup or delivery. However, certain controlled substances may require in-person visits as per regulations.

What types of medical conditions can be treated virtually?

MediMeet is suitable for many non-emergency conditions including cold and flu, allergies, skin conditions, minor infections, follow-up care, chronic disease management, mental health consultations, and more. Emergency conditions requiring immediate physical examination should be treated at an emergency room.

How do I become a healthcare provider on MediMeet?

Licensed healthcare providers can join MediMeet by completing our verification process. This includes credential verification, background checks, and platform training. Visit the Doctor's Panel to start your application and learn more about our provider opportunities.
